🎒 What to Bring to Nursery

Starting nursery is a big and exciting milestone for both children and parents. To ensure your child feels safe, comfortable, and ready to enjoy their day, it’s important to come prepared with a few everyday essentials. From spare clothes to their favourite comfort item, each item plays a part in helping them settle in and feel at home. This guide will help you pack everything your little one needs — so they can focus on learning, playing, and making new friends, while you enjoy peace of mind knowing they have all they need.

Recommended Items

  • Spare Clothes – In case of spills or accidents. Please label all items.
  • Nappies and Wipes – If your child still needs them.
  • Comfort Item – A favourite toy or blanket for reassurance.
  • Weather-Appropriate Clothing – Such as hats, gloves, or sunhats depending on the season.
  • Water Bottle – Clearly labelled with your child’s name.
  • Lunch Box / Snacks – If not provided by the nursery (please check with us).
  • Wellies and Outdoor Gear – For outdoor play and muddy adventures.

What Not to Bring

  • Valuable or sentimental toys – We can’t guarantee their safety or prevent loss/damage.
  • Electronic devices – Such as tablets, mobile phones, or smartwatches are not allowed.
  • Food containing nuts or allergens – To ensure the safety of children with allergies.
  • Sharp objects or small choking hazards – Safety is our top priority.
  • Medications without prior arrangement – Please speak to staff before sending any medication.
  • Money or personal items – These are not necessary and may get lost.

Essential Extras for a Smooth Nursery Day

Preparing your child for nursery goes beyond the basics — it’s about making sure they feel secure, supported, and ready to enjoy their day. Below are a few extra essentials that can make a big difference in your child’s comfort, learning, and happiness while they’re with us.

🧸 Comfort Items

Helping Them Feel at Home

Bring a small, familiar toy or blanket that helps your child feel secure throughout the day.

👕 Spare Clothing

Prepared for Little Accidents

Pack extra clothes in case of spills or messes, and label each item clearly with your child’s name.

🧴 Nappies & Wipes

For Our Youngest Learners

If your child is not yet toilet trained, please include enough nappies and wipes for the day.

🧢 Weather Gear

Dress for the Day Ahead

Provide hats, coats, gloves, or sunhats so your child is ready for any weather during outdoor play.

🥤 Water Bottle

Staying Hydrated Matters

A clearly labelled bottle helps your child stay refreshed and encourages healthy hydration habits.

🍱 Meals & Snacks

Healthy Food from Home

If meals aren’t provided, pack nutritious, nut-free food your child can easily manage on their own.
